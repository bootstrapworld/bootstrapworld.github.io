Creating a "Filter" Transformer

Open the Transformers plugin, and select Filter.

1 Enter a filter-is-fixed as the name. What does this name tell you about the Transformer you are creating?

2 Which dataset should we select, to apply this Transformer?

3 Examine the contract. Why does the provided contract make sense?

4 Write a purpose statement.

  • Enter Fixed = true as the expression.

5 Select Apply Transformer. What happens?

6 Try typing fixed = true as the expression (instead of Fixed = true). What happens?

7 What happens when you enter FIXED = true? How about Fixed = TRUE?

More Filtering

8 Create, save, and apply a Transformer called filter-is-old that creates a new dataset with animals older than 5 years. Note that Age (years) needs to be typed within backticks ` ` in order for CODAP to recognize it as an attribute. (The backtick key is in the upper left corner of the keyboard.)
