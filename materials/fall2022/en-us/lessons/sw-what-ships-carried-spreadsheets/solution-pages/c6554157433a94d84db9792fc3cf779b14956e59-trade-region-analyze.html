Analyzing Trade Regions

1 Make a copy of Shipwreck Trade Region Data (Google).

2 Follow the directions in the file to create a pie chart of the data.

Read off the chart to answer the following questions:

3 Which regions traded the most?

4 Which region was involved in more of the routes: the Netherlands or India?

Knowing what the ships carried tells us what people wanted to buy and sell. It doesn’t tell us why those items were valuable.
