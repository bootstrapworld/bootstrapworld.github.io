Exploring the Shipwreck Evidence Cards

You and your partner(s) should have a set of Shipwreck Evidence Cards. The front of each card names a shipwreck. The back of each card lists the regions that the ship was traveling between and what the ship was carrying. Look at the items listed on the cards.

1 Which items come up on multiple cards?

2 What percentage of the ships carried ceramics? Remember that we determine percentage by dividing the number of ships that carried ceramics by the total number of ships.

Now look at the regions mentioned on the cards (They are in green letters).

3 Which regions do you see mentioned?

4 Which regions were most active in trade based on the cards?
