CODAP Exploration

Make sure you’re logged into the Animals Starter File in CODAP. The purpose of this exploration is to familiarize yourself with some of CODAP’s features. Feel free to experiment and try things out!

1 See if you can make a graph. What did you select in order to do so?

I clicked on the "Graph" icon in the upper left-hand corner of my screen

2 Move the table. Move the graph. How do you move things in CODAP?

To move tables and graphs, I mouse over the top-most bar until my cursor turns into a hand. Then I can drag.

3 Minimize and maximize the table and the graph. How do you do that?

To minimize, I select the - in the upper right-hand corner of the graph or table. To maximize, I reselect the -.

4 Drag an attribute (like weight, name, or sex) to a graph’s axis - or use the drop-down menu that appears when you click on an axis. What happens?

The randomly positioned points move to the confirguration that matches the data on the animals table.

5 Drag a second attribute (like weight, name, or sex) to the graph’s other axis - or use the drop-down menu that appears when you click on an axis. What happens?

The points move again, to the confirguration indicated by the two attributes on the animals table.

6 Select the grey plus sign (+) in the upper right-hand corner of the Animals Table. Doing so will enable you to create a new attribute. Name your new attribute test. Is the column populated or empty?

The column is empty.
