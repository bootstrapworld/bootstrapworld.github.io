Writing Examples from Purpose Statements 2

We’ve provided contracts and purpose statements to describe two different functions. Write examples for each of those functions.

Directions: half-image

Contract and Purpose Statement

Every contract has three parts…​

half-image: String->Image

Consumes an image, and produces that image scaled to half its size.

Examples

Write some examples, then circle and label what changes…​

(EXAMPLE (half-image "code.pyret.org/img/pyret-logo.png")(scale 0.5 (image-url "code.pyret.org/img/pyret-logo.png")))

(EXAMPLE (half-image "bootstrapworld.org/images/icon.png")(scale 0.5 (image-url "bootstrapworld.org/images/icon.png")))

Definition

Write the definition, giving variable names to all your input values…​

(define (half-image url)

__(scale 0.5 (image-url url)))

Directions: product-squared

Contract and Purpose Statement

Every contract has three parts…​

product-squared: Number Number->Number

Consumes two numbers and squares their product

Examples

Write some examples, then circle and label what changes…​

(EXAMPLE (product-squared 5 6)(sqr (* 5 6)))

(EXAMPLE (product-squared 10 17)(sqr (* 10 17)))

Definition

Write the definition, giving variable names to all your input values…​

(define (product-squared a b)

__(sqr (* a b)))
