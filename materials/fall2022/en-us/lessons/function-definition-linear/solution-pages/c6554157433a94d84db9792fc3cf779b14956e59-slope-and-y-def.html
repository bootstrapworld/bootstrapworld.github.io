Identifying Slope and y-intercept in Definitions

The following function definitions are written in math notation and in Pyret. Can you identify their slope and y-intercept?

1

fx = 3/4x + 19

slope/rate: 3/4

y-intercept: 19

2

fun c(d) = (7.5 * d) + 22 end

slope/rate: 7.5

y-intercept: 22

3

fun g(h): 20 - (16 * h) end

slope/rate: -16

y-intercept: 20

4

gx = 91 + 4x

slope/rate: 4

y-intercept: 91

5

fun i(j): -15 + (1.5 * j) end

slope/rate: 1.5

y-intercept: -15

6

hx = 10x - 2/5

slope/rate: 10

y-intercept: - 2/5
